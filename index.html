Research

I consider myself a "Cosmic Meteorologist". Our research group, led by Irina Zhuravleva, observes and simulates motions in the intra-cluster medium (ICM). This is a diffuse plasma that surrounds clusters of galaxies, with temperatures reaching up to 100 million degrees. We use X-ray telescopes to study cold fronts, shocks, bubbles, and turbulence in these cluster atmospheres. In the past I have studied simulated interactions between AGN jets and the ICM. Currently I am mostly interested in the generation of turbulence by hierarchical cluster mergers.
